Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our services, approach, or how we can support your business?

What industries do you serve?

We primarily serve financial institutions, NBFCs, fintech companies, and corporates looking for technology-driven process transformation and operational efficiency.

How does Accelbiz Fintech use technology in its services?

We utilize advanced technologies such as AI, RPA, cloud platforms, and automation tools (like Automation Anywhere) to deliver scalable, secure, and efficient business solutions.

Can you customize solutions for specific business needs?

Absolutely. We adopt a collaborative and agile approach, working closely with clients to understand their needs and co-create customized solutions.

Do you provide support for customer onboarding and credit processing?

Yes. Our back-office services include customer onboarding, credit processing, profile verification, reconciliation, and transaction settlements across various loan products.

How does your Robotic Process Automation (RPA) help businesses?

Our RPA solutions automate repetitive, rule-based tasks, enhancing speed, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness in operations.

What is your approach to data security and compliance?

We prioritize data security and compliance by implementing robust security protocols and adhering to industry regulations, including data masking and secure onboarding processes.
